Ritual

This is the ritual.

As soon as your pet gets to Step 1, you should get them started on this.

It is an extremely important piece of this programme, because it is vital to getting to break your pet.

Everytime you see them, it is fundamental they repeat this ritual, so they get accustomed to being your pet. They should repeat, at least 5-10 times:

"I, <name>, am <the name you chose for your pet> and property of <your name>, <name you chose for yourself>, and I am grateful."

Then kiss one of your shoes and start over.
